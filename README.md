## What It Does

AI Video Dubbing Studio is an iOS app that uses AI to transform video audio. Pick a video from your library, choose an AI mode, select a voice, and export — all on-device with no watermark.

### Four AI Modes

| Mode | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| **[Voice Changer](https://viongen.com/voice-changer.html)** | Replace the voice in any video with one of 70+ AI voices. Same language, new voice. |
| **[Dubbing Engine](https://viongen.com/dubbing-engine.html)** | Translate & dub videos into 67 languages with accent-aware voice synthesis. |
| **[Subtitle Studio](https://viongen.com/subtitle-studio.html)** | Generate subtitles automatically. Edit, translate, export SRT/VTT, or burn into video. |
| **[Auto Narrate](https://viongen.com/auto-narrate.html)** | AI analyzes scenes frame-by-frame, writes a narration script, and dubs it with a chosen voice. |

## Features

- 70+ AI voices across 3 quality tiers (Standard, Expressive, Ultra)
- 67 languages with accent-aware synthesis
- Karaoke-style word-by-word subtitle highlighting
- SRT and VTT subtitle file export
- Deep segment editor — edit text, regenerate audio per segment
- Export in original resolution, no watermark
- iPhone and iPad (iOS 18.1+)

## How It Works

1. **Import** — Pick any video from your library or Files app
2. **Choose a Mode** — Voice Changer, Dubbing Engine, Subtitle Studio, or Auto Narrate
3. **Select a Voice** — Browse 70+ voices with audio previews
4. **Generate** — AI transcribes, translates (if dubbing), and synthesizes new audio
5. **Edit** — Fine-tune with the deep segment editor
6. **Export** — Save in original quality or share directly
